# Security

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability in Better Blood (this repository or the app at app.betterblood.ai), please report it responsibly.

**Do not** open a public GitHub issue for security-sensitive bugs.

Instead:

1. **Email** the maintainers with a description of the issue and steps to reproduce. You can reach us via the contact information on [betterblood.ai](https://betterblood.ai).
2. We will acknowledge your report and work with you to understand and address it.
3. We will not take legal action against or ask law enforcement to investigate researchers who report vulnerabilities in good faith and make a reasonable effort to avoid privacy violations and disruption.

We appreciate your help in keeping Better Blood and its users safe.
